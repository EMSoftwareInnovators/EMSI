About us

Beginning in early 2015, the original concept for EM Software innovators came from a computer programming meetup in a small town in Georgia. Although the meetup declined in patrons over the following months, the ones who did stay were devoted to learning how to make interesting and innovative computer programs and mobile apps that would change the world. Thus, EM Software Innovators was born.
